Privacy Policy for Vizique

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vizique ("we", "our", or "us") processes information when you use the Vizique mobile application.

1. Overview

Vizique is designed to minimize data collection. However, certain data is processed to provide core functionality, including image-based analysis and advertising.

2. Information We Process

2.1 User-Provided Data

  • Physique photos uploaded by users
  • Fitness-related inputs (e.g., height, weight, goals)

This data is used solely to generate analysis results.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

Vizique does not implement analytics, tracking, or user accounts. However, third-party advertising services may collect device-level information as described below.

3. How Data Is Processed

When you upload a photo:

  • The image and related inputs are transmitted securely (HTTPS) to Vizique-controlled servers
  • The image is temporarily stored on the server for processing
  • The image is analyzed using locally hosted processing tools
  • The image file is deleted after processing is completed
  • Analysis results are returned to your device

Vizique does not use submitted data for training, profiling, or secondary purposes.

Important: While images are intended to be deleted immediately after processing, temporary files may persist in rare cases such as unexpected errors or system interruptions.

4. Local Storage

Data generated by the app is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Analysis results
  • Workout recommendations
  • Preferences and settings

This data may be included in device backups (such as iCloud or Google Drive), depending on your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

Vizique uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA or Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

Google may use this information for personalized or non-personalized advertising in accordance with its own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

  • Vizique does not maintain persistent user data on its servers
  • Images are temporarily stored only during processing and then deleted
  • Data stored locally remains until you delete the app
  • Third-party services may retain data according to their own policies

7. User Control

  • You may delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • You may manage ad personalization through your device settings

8. Security

We use secure transmission (HTTPS) to protect data in transit. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s Privacy

Vizique is not intended for users under the age of 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed on servers located outside your country.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
