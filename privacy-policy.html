Privacy Policy for Vizique

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Vizique ("Vizique," "we," "our," or "us") processes, handles, and protects information related to your use of the Vizique mobile application (the "App").

Vizique is intentionally designed with strict privacy-by-design principles and collects no personal information except what is required for non-personalized advertising.

1. Definitions

  • “Personal Information” means information that identifies or relates to an individual. Vizique does not collect Personal Information for app usage or analytics.
  • “Ad Data” means limited technical information automatically collected by Google AdMob strictly for serving non-personalized ads.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Aside from technical data automatically processed by Google AdMob for non-personalized ads, Vizique does not collect:

  • Personal Information
  • Health or fitness metrics
  • Photos or user-generated content
  • Contact information
  • Behavioral profiles or interest-based data
  • Crash logs or usage analytics
  • Metadata associated with your images

Any data processed by AdMob is controlled by Google and not stored or accessed by Vizique.

6. AdMob & Non-Personalized Advertising

Vizique displays advertisements using the Google AdMob SDK. Vizique has configured AdMob to serve non-personalized ads only to all users, regardless of age, region, or device settings.

Non-personalized ads do not use behavioral, interest-based, demographic, or cross-app data. No profiling or tracking is performed.

6.1 Information Processed by Google AdMob

To serve non-personalized ads, Google may automatically process limited technical information, which may include:

  • IP address (used for coarse location only)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App instance identifiers necessary to deliver ads
  • General ad performance metrics

Vizique does not receive, store, or access this information.

6.2 No Personalized Advertising

  • No behavior-based targeting
  • No interest categories or demographic targeting
  • No personalized ad profiles or identifiers
  • No cross-app tracking or remarketing
  • No sale or sharing of data with third parties by Vizique

6.3 Google as Independent Data Controller

For any data automatically processed by Google, Google acts as an independent data controller in accordance with its own privacy policies:

“Google independently determines the purposes and means of processing necessary to deliver non-personalized ads.”
