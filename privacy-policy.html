Privacy Policy for Vizique

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Vizique ("Vizique," "we," "our," or "us") processes, handles, and protects information related to your use of the Vizique mobile application (the "App").

Vizique is intentionally designed with strict privacy-by-design principles and collects no personal information except what is automatically processed by Google AdMob for non-personalized advertising.

1. Definitions

  • “Personal Information” means information that identifies, relates to, or can reasonably be associated with an individual. Vizique does not collect Personal Information for app functionality or analytics.
  • “Processing” means any operation performed on data, including collection, transmission, temporary analysis, or deletion.
  • “Temporary Processing Data” refers to images, numbers, or other inputs voluntarily provided by the user for real-time computation, which are immediately discarded after use.
  • “iCloud Storage” refers to Apple’s Key-Value storage system associated with your Apple ID and managed entirely by Apple.
  • “Ad Data” means limited technical data processed automatically by Google AdMob solely for delivering non-personalized ads.
  • “User” or “You” means any individual who downloads, installs, or uses Vizique.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Apart from technical data processed by Google AdMob for non-personalized ads, Vizique does not collect, store, or access:

  • Personal Information
  • Health or fitness metrics
  • Photos, images, or user-generated content
  • Contact information
  • Behavioral or interest-based tracking data
  • Device identifiers used for profiling
  • Crash logs or analytics
  • Metadata associated with uploaded images

Vizique does not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

3. Temporary Processing

Some App features require temporary processing of data you choose to provide. You acknowledge and agree that:

  • Your data is transmitted securely over encrypted channels (HTTPS).
  • Processing occurs only in volatile memory and is never written to persistent storage.
  • Temporary Processing Data is deleted immediately once results are produced.
  • No logs, caches, or backups of this data are created.

This data handling is necessary solely for performing the requested functionality.

4. Data Stored on Your Device and in iCloud

All App-generated outputs—including workout summaries, settings, and similar information—are stored either locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account if enabled.

Vizique does not have access to:

  • Your Apple ID
  • Any data stored in your iCloud account
  • Any files or information synced between your devices

All iCloud-related processing is controlled independently by Apple.

5. iCloud Synchronization

When iCloud is enabled, certain lightweight App data (text, preferences, summaries) may sync across your Apple devices. Syncing:

  • Does not include photos, images, or workout data
  • Is handled exclusively by Apple
  • Is never accessible by Vizique

6. AdMob & Non-Personalized Advertising

Vizique displays advertisements using the Google AdMob SDK. Vizique has configured AdMob to serve non-personalized ads only to all users, worldwide, without exception.

Non-personalized ads do not use behavioral tracking, demographic targeting, or cross-app activity.

6.1 Information Processed by Google AdMob

To serve non-personalized ads, Google may automatically process limited technical information, including:

  • IP address (for coarse region determination)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App instance identifiers required for ad delivery
  • Ad performance and diagnostic information

Vizique does not collect, store, or access this information.

6.2 No Personalized Advertising

Vizique strictly prohibits:

  • Behavioral targeting
  • Interest-based personalization
  • Demographic targeting
  • User profiling or segmentation
  • Cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • Sale or sharing of user information

6.3 Google as Independent Data Controller

Google determines the means and purposes of processing Ad Data under its own privacy policies. Vizique does not control, access, or store this information.

7. Security

Although Vizique does not store user data on its servers, we implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encrypted communication for all App requests
  • In-memory-only processing architecture
  • No external analytics or tracking scripts

AdMob communications are also encrypted as required by Google.

8. Children’s Privacy

Vizique does not knowingly collect any Personal Information from children under 13. All ads shown to minors are automatically served as non-personalized under AdMob’s child-directed processing requirements.

9. Data Persistence, Retention, and Removal

Persistent data exists only on your device or in your personal iCloud account. Deleting the App removes local data but not iCloud data.

You may delete iCloud-stored data through your Apple device settings. Vizique cannot access or modify iCloud data.

10. CCPA / CPRA Compliance

Because Vizique does not collect or store Personal Information:

  • No Personal Information is sold or shared
  • No opt-out mechanisms are required
  • No access, correction, or deletion requests apply to Vizique

Google may process limited Ad Data as described above, independently under its own legal obligations.

11. International Data Transfers

Vizique does not store any user information. However, AdMob’s infrastructure may process limited technical data in global data centers, controlled solely by Google.

No data is transferred, stored, or controlled by Vizique internationally.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
